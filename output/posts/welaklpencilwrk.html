Wellington and Auckland pencil works

Posted: 2013-12-09 13:31 [Source]

It's been a long time since I've uploaded any pencil drawings to the site. During my recent visit to Wellington I picked up a new A5 sketchbook that I have been busy filling with pencil drawings. I also have a 4H pencil which is my favorite, certainly more enjoyable than HB pencil to use.

Much of these drawings are from photo reference, with some from photo and life.

I am offering digital painting portraits for cheap.
